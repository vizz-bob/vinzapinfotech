Our Story

About Vinzap Infotech

A passionate IT company from Bangalore helping students and professionals build real, lasting careers in technology since 2019.

Who We Are

Built in Bangalore, Trusted Worldwide

Vinzap Infotech was founded in 2019 with a single, clear mission: to make professional IT education accessible to everyone — regardless of their background or geography. We started as a small team of DevOps engineers and digital marketers who were frustrated by the gap between what colleges teach and what companies actually need.

Today, we are a full-service IT company and training institute based in Bangalore, India. We serve clients across IT services, cloud consulting, web development, and digital marketing, while simultaneously training 500+ students every year through our online programs designed for learners in India, the USA, the UK, Australia, and Canada.

Every course we create, every tutorial we publish, and every service we deliver is guided by one belief: real skills lead to real careers. We don't just teach theory — we build projects, simulate real workplaces, and mentor students until they land the job.

Our instructors are practicing professionals with 5–15 years of hands-on experience at companies like Infosys, Wipro, and AWS partners. They bring the classroom to life with real pipelines, real cloud architectures, and real marketing campaigns — not slides and bullet points.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Purpose

Our Mission

To provide accessible, industry-aligned IT education and professional services that equip every learner with the practical skills they need to thrive in a technology-driven world. We believe learning should lead to employment — not just certificates.

🔭

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted technology training and services company, producing 10,000 job-ready DevOps engineers and digital marketers by 2030 — from every city, town, and village with an internet connection.
