Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get asked most often.

Are your courses available online? ▼
Yes, all our courses are 100% online. We deliver live sessions via Zoom and also provide recorded videos so you can learn at your own pace. Students from India, USA, UK, Australia, Canada, and other countries are actively enrolled.
Do you offer EMI or installment payment? ▼
Yes. We offer flexible payment plans for all our programs. You can split your course fee into 2–3 installments. Contact us via WhatsApp or the form above to discuss options suited to your situation.
What is the minimum qualification to join? ▼
Most of our programs are open to anyone with basic computer knowledge. You don't need a computer science degree. Our DevOps and AWS courses start from absolute zero — we teach Linux basics before moving into cloud and CI/CD concepts.
Do you provide job placement assistance? ▼
Yes, job placement assistance is included with every course. This includes resume review, LinkedIn profile optimization, mock interviews, and direct referrals to our hiring partner network. Our current placement rate is 95%.
How can I get a course brochure or detailed syllabus? ▼
Simply fill in the contact form above or message us on WhatsApp. We'll send you the full course syllabus, schedule, pricing, and any current offers within a few hours.
